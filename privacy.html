Introduction

At NBR accountant, we respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting their data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the application.

Information We Collect

The application collects the minimum information necessary to provide the core service, including:

How We Use Information

Data is used solely for:

  • Activating the application and linking it to the user's account.

We do not use user data for advertising, tracking, or marketing analytics purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Data is stored using secure and encrypted cloud storage services. We take reasonable measures to protect data from unauthorized access, modification, or loss.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with any third parties. External services may be used solely to provide cloud storage without accessing the content of your data.

Data Deletion

Users can delete their data at any time by removing the stored backups or by contacting the developer to request deletion of data associated with their account.

Children's Privacy

The application is not intended for children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
